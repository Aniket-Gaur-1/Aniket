About Me

A bit more about my journey, passions, and what drives me.

Hi, I'm Aniket Gaur—a passionate MERN stack developer focused on building clean, scalable full-stack web applications. I enjoy turning ideas into practical digital solutions using React, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB, with an emphasis on usability and real-world problem solving.

My development approach focuses on simplicity, clarity, and maintainable code. I take inspiration from modern web practices and strive to build interfaces that are intuitive, responsive, and efficient. When I’m not coding, I spend time learning new technologies, improving my problem-solving skills, and experimenting with UI improvements in my projects.
